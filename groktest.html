About Our Puja

The Palpara Sarbojoni Jagadhatri Puja is one of the oldest and most revered community celebrations in Chandannagar, with a rich history spanning over eight decades.

Our Origin

Established in 1944, our puja began in the front yard of Harihar Seth Mahashay's house, initiated by energetic youth and supported by prominent community members.

Community Spirit

The festival thrives on the active participation of local residents who consider it an integral part of their cultural identity and heritage.

Cultural Significance

While maintaining traditional rituals, the festival has evolved to include cultural programs and social initiatives that engage all generations.

Our Journey

A timeline of our rich history and significant milestones over the past eight decades.

1944

The Beginning

The inception of Palpara Sarbojoni Jagadhatri Puja in the front yard of Harihar Seth Mahashay's house.

1960s

Growing Popularity

The puja gains popularity and becomes a significant community event in Chandannagar.

1980s

Cultural Expansion

Introduction of cultural programs and competitions alongside the religious ceremonies.

2000s

Modernization

Modernization of celebrations while preserving traditional rituals and values.

2024

Grand Celebrations

Celebrating 80 glorious years with grand festivities and community participation.
